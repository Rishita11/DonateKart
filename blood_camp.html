Blood Donation Camp

About The Event

This blood donation camp is being organised by rotary blood bank situated at sector-37 A Chandigarh. People are encouraged to donate blood to provide blood for people affected by medical conditions and emergency/routine procedures as well.This event also aims to raise awareness regarding common misconceptions regarding blood donation and common medical myths.



AIM

The primary aim is to collect blood to provide it to people suffering from medical conditions and requiring medical aid. This event also aims to raise awareness regarding blood donation and common misbeliefs regarding the same amongst common people.



ARE U ELIGIBLE?

  1. U should be 18-65 years of age
  2. U should be more than 45 kg weight
  3. Should NOT have any heart diseases
  4. Should NOT have Hepatitis B, C, Tuberculosis, Leprosy, HIV.
  5. Should NOT have cancer.
